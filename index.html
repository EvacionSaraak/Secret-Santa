How Secret Santa Works:
  • Select your name from the participants list to begin.
  • Click on any available box to claim it and see who you're gifting to!
  • Important: Once you select a box, you cannot change your selection. Choose carefully!
  • Once you select a box, you'll see the name of the person you should buy a gift for.
  • To change your name: Click the "Change Name" button next to your name at the top. This updates your identity without affecting your box selection.
  • Privacy: Only you can see who you're gifting to. Others only see "Claimed" on boxes.
  • Note: Data is stored for a maximum of 1 week due to free tier limitations. Complete your event within this timeframe.
